Abstract

This ontology covers the classes and properties for dating the poetic opus and its manifestations. This ontology is one of the ontologies in the POSTDATA ontology network, a network of ontologies for European poetry.

The description of the ontology network can be consulted through this link and all the documentation relating to the development of the ontology network is accessible at https://github.com/linhd-postdata/Network-of-ontologies.

The core-ontology documentation is accesible at https://github.com/linhd-postdata/date-ontology.

This development is open to receive suggestions and problems found by the community of users and the research community through https://github.com/linhd-postdata/core-ontology/issues.
